# Brawlight Manor
Brawlight Manor is a competitive 1v1 game set in a dark and mysterious mansion where light is your most valuable resource.

Choose the character that fits your style the most and start exploring the map to find and capture crystals while facing your opponent in close combat. Use your flashlight to reveal the environment, control key areas and gain the upper hand during fights.
